CYCLO BONETTE

CYCLO BONETTE left Beirut at the age of 18 looking for freedom from the culture's harshness towards queer people. After four years of Deejaying at DIY events and bars, they started producing from their narrow bedroom in New York City. They use middle eastern beats to intertwine centuries of tradition with fast paced, brain scratching heavy kicks and unserious breaks creating a high energy spiritual musical experience. The soundscape they create is designed for the soul to freely swim in, one which rules of traditional electronic music are left behind. Their attraction to freedom is clearly reflected by their boundless music where a BPM limit does not seem to come in the way.
